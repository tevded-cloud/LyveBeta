## LDNOOBW English Moderation Lexicon

Lyve Chat's built-in severe-slur protection uses a manually reviewed, high-confidence subset derived from the English list in the **List of Dirty, Naughty, Obscene, and Otherwise Bad Words** project.

- Source: https://github.com/LDNOOBW/List-of-Dirty-Naughty-Obscene-and-Otherwise-Bad-Words

The source list is broader than Lyve Chat's use case and includes general profanity, sexual language, and context-sensitive terms. Lyve Chat does not import the complete list. Its built-in immediate-ban rules are limited to a conservative subset of high-confidence protected-class slurs, with additional normalization for common spacing and character substitutions.
